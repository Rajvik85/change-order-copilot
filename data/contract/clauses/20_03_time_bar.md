# Clause 20.3 — Time Bar

If the initial notice required by Clause 20.1 is served after 14 calendar days,
entitlement is time-barred unless the receiving party expressly waives the bar
in writing. A missing notice date is treated as at risk pending evidence, not
as proof of compliance.

Detailed particulars identifying the basis, records, quantum, and schedule
effect shall follow within 28 calendar days after awareness. For a continuing
event, interim monthly updates preserve transparency without extending the
initial notice limit.
