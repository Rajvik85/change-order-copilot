# Clause 4.7 — Differing Physical Conditions

If the Contractor encounters a concealed physical condition materially
different from information reasonably available at the Base Date, it shall
protect the work, keep contemporaneous records, and notify under Clause 20.1.
Demonstrable additional Cost and critical delay may be valued after comparison
with the condition a competent contractor should reasonably have anticipated.
